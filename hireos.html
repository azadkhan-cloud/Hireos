The problem

Most recruitment desks run on five disconnected tools

A spreadsheet for the pipeline. A CRM for clients. Email for candidates. WhatsApp for feedback. Tally for invoices. Nothing talks to anything — so nothing is ever really visible.

🧩

Fragmented data

The same candidate exists in four places, and none of them agree.

🕳️

Pipelines go quiet

Candidates sit untouched for a week and nobody notices until the client asks.

📧

Status-update tax

Recruiters spend their week telling clients what the system should be showing them.

💸

Revenue leaks

Placements made, invoices forgotten, guarantee follow-ups missed.
