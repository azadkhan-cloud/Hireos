How we work

From brief to joined — with the lights on

You see every stage in real time through your client portal. No black box, no chasing.

Intake & calibration

We map the role, the must-haves and the realistic market rate before we source a single CV.

AI-assisted sourcing

Boolean and X-ray sourcing plus our internal database. Every candidate is AI-scored against your JD.

Structured screening

Human screening on top of AI scoring — notice period, comp, intent, fit. Only real contenders reach you.

Offer & onboarding

Interviews coordinated, offers managed, documents and BGV tracked through joining. Then 90 days of follow-up.
